About

Who?

Forming in 2014, the team behind SPLITelevision Productions committed ourselves to experimenting and exploring the various mediums, genres, and styles of storytelling that are available to us. This has provided an opportunity for us to work with people trained in many different areas of the entertainment industry to produce a wide range of what we fondly refer to as… 'content'.

In 2019, as part of Fringe Festival NZ, our team have written, devised, produced, and are performing our very first original stage show. You can find out more about the Characters and the Cast & Crew, who have helped make 'Yesterday, In Space' a possibility, on other pages of the site, once it's fully online.

 

What?

‘Yesterday, In Space’ combines sci-fi, soap opera, and musical theatre to create an immersive stage show that examines diverse characters, explores the galaxy above our homes, and has fun with the tropes that people come to expect and enjoy from these genres.

As an audience member you will be welcomed aboard the Yesterday, New Zealand’s very first space-cruise ship. During our sixty-minute journey there will be flashing lights, original musical numbers, and queer, kiwi comedy. You have been warned.

 

When?

This show is ONLY being performed thrice! 7:30PM on Monday 18th, Tuesday 19th, and Wednesday 20th of March 2019. There is limited seating available, so please book your tickets before the space-cruise takes off without you!

 

Why?

The Fringe Festival has a history of being the perfect platform for new writers, singers, actors, and performers to showcase or debut themselves to the performing arts community. This seemed like the perfect opportunity for the members of SPLITelevision Productions to come together, create, and collaborate on something brand-new.

They have taken three immensely popular genres, combined them with their favourite and least favourite tropes, and wrapped them into a sixty minute show with acapella soundscapes and seven original musical numbers.

We hope that 'Yesterday, In Space' will entertain, show diversity of culture, queerness, and hopefully encourage people to stop focussing on the successes and struggles of yesterday and to begin looking forward to the opportunities and obstacles of tomorrow, today.

 

Where?

Gryphon Theatre, 22 Ghuznee Street, Te Aro, Wellington.

Thanks to FatG (Fringe at the Gryphon), 'Yesterday, In Space' has been given a three-night season to debut our show at the Gryphon Theatre! It's a five minute walk from Cuba Street so come on down! If you're bringing a vehicle then it's best to use the Leeds Street Carpark.

 

How?

Producing and performing an original stage show would not have been possible without assistance from FatG, Gryphon Theatre, and Fringe Festival NZ who have provided us with the venue and festival to showcase our work to the people of Aotearoa.

There have been many individuals, businesses, and groups who have supported or assisted with this show in some way, and a full list can be found at the bottom of the Cast & Crew page once the full site is online. However, we would like to use this space to thank the Kākano Panel for choosing 'Yesterday, In Space' to receive a financial grant towards our production expenses (sponsored by Creative NZ), as well as The Menagerie Variety Show grant towards Marketing (sponsored by The Menagerie Variety Show).

Finally, a massive thanks to all friends, whanau, cast, and crew of SPLITelevision Productions who have truly proven and furthered themselves as artists and performers during this creative process and have come together to collaborate and create a truly wonderful show.
